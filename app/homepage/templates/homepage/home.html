Who Are We?

Transfr allows you to easily schedule rides to and from medical appointments within the Oxford, MS area. We pair you with qualified drivers who are well-trained in safe transportation services and guaranteed to get to your destination on time.

How Does It Work?

Create an account or log in, and then click the 'Request a Ride' tab. Fill out the form, and we will assign a driver to you for your scheduled day and time.

What Does It Cost?

Pricing will be managed through your insurance. No co-pay required.
